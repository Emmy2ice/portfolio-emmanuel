About

Having graduated as an Animal and Environmental Biologist from the University of Benin, Benin City, Nigeria, in 2016, I was fortunate to secure an employement with then Cars45 Nig Ltd in 2019 as a sales manager before moving on to work with Zenith bank Nigeria Plc in 2020. All this while, I always felt the urge to be part of the growing community of tech experts, especially as I had a reputation for proferring solutions to real life problems - something I feel is a natural attribute for most tech experts. So, in 2021, I enroled with the STUTERN ACADEMY (even though at that point, I was still employed with Zenith Bank Nigeria Plc). It was an extremely tasking venture, learning web engineering and working as a banker same time. In the end, thanks to determination, I was able to develop myself, under their tutelage, to gain basic knowledge to kickstart my journey to attaining incredible heights in the world of front-end web developement.

I am proficient working with HTML, CSS (including Bootstrap) and JAVASCRIPT (with good enough knowledge of REACT.JS). I am also very good working in a team to create wonderful things within stipulated time periods.

My philosophy in life is that learning should never stop. I believe it is my responsibility to give myself as much education as I can while keeping things positive at all times. I really love front-end engineering and I am always going to keep looking for more opportunities to keep getting better at it.

MY PROJECTS

1. Cloned Stutern Site

My very first major project - a cloned site of the STUTERN ACADEMY official webpage - was a task given me to basically test my understanding of HTML and CSS.

2. Shopping "Add To Cart"

In trying to better my skills and test my understanding of JAVASCRIPT and the DOM, I designed a simple shopping cart site which simply added selected items to a cart list.

3. Demo Login Page

This is a well designed simple but functional demo login page.

4. Fine App

I designed this project using REACT.JS, bootstrap. The project calls an API that searches up the posters of any movie title inputted by the user.
